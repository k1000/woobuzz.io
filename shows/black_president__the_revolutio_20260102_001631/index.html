Black President: The Revolutionary Sound of Fela Kuti

A celebration of Fela Anikulapo Kuti, the Nigerian musician who invented Afrobeat and used his music as a weapon against corruption and military dictatorship. This show explores his incredible journey from London jazz student to Africa's most challenging and charismatic performer.
